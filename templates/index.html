About The Project

This program allows you to make AND upload a highlight video with the push of a button. No need to spend time finding footage, editing it together, or even going to your Youtube studio.

In regards to attribution, all clips contain a text overlay which shows the name of the content creator. A link to the channel of each content creator that appears in the video is also provided with the default description.

With this software you could hypothetically mass-manufacture highlight videos across many verticals and monetize through Google ad revenue and sponsor activations



Simply put, here is how it works:
  1. The X most popular clips for the selected game are downloaded
  2. Clips are "watermarked" with their respective content creator
  3. Clips are then merged together with a quality transition and consolidated into a single .mp4 file
  4. This .mp4 file is uploaded to your youtube channel with the selected options
